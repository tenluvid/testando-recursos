Conheça o grupo Wayv

Novo grupo chinês formada pela SM Entertainment!

WayV (威神V, WeiShen V) is a 6-member Chinese boy group, formed under Label V, a sub-label of SM Entertainment. WayV debuted on the 17th of January 2019 and consists of members Kun (錕), Ten (李永钦), Winwin (昀昀), Xiaojun (肖俊), Hendery (黄冠亨) and Yangyang (扬扬). Former member Lucas (黃旭熙) left the group on May 10, 2023.

Fandom name: WayZenNi / Weishennie

Fandom Colour: Lime/"Neo Pearl Champagne" (to be more specific)

Official Lightstick: Leekbong/"Green Onion Nightstick"

WayV stands for: We are your Vision. 威神V (pinyin: WeiShenV) is a bit different in Chinese, but still just as powerful. 威 means “elite” or “prestige” in Mandarin and 神 means “god” or “heavenly body”. Put together, the literal translation is “elite or prestigious gods”, but a more proper interpretation can be “powerful beings”, or a related concept. Either way, “we are your vision” is similar to acknowledging the existence of a higher being, and aspiring to be the best, fullest, and most powerful version of yourself and your dreams.

Apresentação dos membros que formam o novo boygroup WAYV:

[KUN]

Kun was born in Fujian, China and is the leader and one of the main vocals of WayV. He has a bachelor's degree in Chinese pop vocal performance from Beijing Contemporary Music Institute and has a wide range of skills. He became a trainee after passing SM's 2015 global auditions in China. He's known for his good cooking, as well as his responsible and considerate behaviour around the members. He has his drivers license, enjoys skydiving, and photography. He writes and produces music as well, uploading it on social media.

[TEN]

Ten was born in Thailand and is also of Chinese descent, which is why he is part of WayV. He joined SM through the global auditions in Bangkok in 2013, and he first debuted in NCT U with 'The 7th Sense'. His Mandarin is getting better and better and he's never afraid to express himself in the language. He speaks a total of four languages fluently and is well-known outside the fandom for his dancing skills. He has his drivers license. Pre-debut, he took part in the Thai show Teen Superstar which he won, the winner's prize being a spot as a trainee at Starship Entertainment, which he refused!

[WINWIN]

Winwin was born in Wenzhou and started his stage training at a young age. He is a student at the Beijing Central Academy of Drama in the acting department and had a focus on traditional Chinese dance at the Beijing Dance Academy, where he got scouted by SM. He first debuted with two NCT units, 127 and U, before re-debuting with WayV. He speaks fluent Mandarin, Wenzhounese and Korean and is learning English. At the moment he has acted in two dramas, 25 Hours of Love, which was released August 2023 and in which he was the male lead, and The Shadow which has yet to be released.

[XIAOJUN]

Xiaojun was born in Guangdong and is from a musical family; his father is active in the Chinese entertainment industry. He is a student of Shanghai Theatre Academy, where his major is musical art. He participated in the survival show X-Fire, co-produced by SM Entertainment, where he was scouted. He was already set to debut when he joined the company at age 18. His timbre, vocal technique and dancing skills leave no doubt as to why this was the case! He's working on his English skills and has done Instagram lives in English in the past.

[HENDERY]

Hendery was born in Macau, so his mother tongue is Cantonese. He was spotted by an SM agent when he was out shopping in Beijing where he was studying acting and thinking about becoming a comedian. On WayV's discography he can be recognised by his deep voice contrasting the main vocals. He has received many different nicknames from Donkey for his wide smile and Cucumber (a Chinese pun) to Prince for his handsome looks. He wrote lyrics to King of Heart and We Go Nanana on Take Over the Moon. He is taking drum lessons, as he thinks it is important to have hobbies!

[YANGYANG]

Yangyang was born in Taiwan and moved to Germany in 2011. He moved to South Korea after passing the SM global auditions in 2016. He is known for his rapping and got highlighted in dance breaks at MAMA 2019 and Zhejiang TV, hinting at his dance skills. He is known for being vocal about his thoughts, creating many funny and surprising moments when he's doing Instagram lives together with Ten. His hobbies include playing the violin, gaming, and he has an interest in fashion. He is brushing up on his German at the moment.
